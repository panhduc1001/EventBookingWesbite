About this event

Every 2 years, the Parc des Expositions de la Porte de Versailles welcomes the Paris Motor Show – an international event dedicated to the world of cars. This year, it is celebrating its 120th anniversary. The Motor Show is the trade show that draws the largest attendance in France, and enthusiasts from all around the world. Programme line-up: a showcase for some 200 brands of car manufacturers, the latest prototypes from major vehicle brands, special events relating to the car industry and much more.

Date and Time

10:00 AM - 6:00PM (GMT+2)
